Bretton Woods · 1944
The Architecture Is Locked In
44 nations meet in New Hampshire as the war is still being fought. Keynes proposes a neutral international currency. The United States insists on the dollar. The US holds two-thirds of the world's gold and is the only industrial economy not destroyed. The dollar wins. Every nation's reconstruction debt flows to Washington. The creditor position becomes the imperial position. This is where the architecture begins.
